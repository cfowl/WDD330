Week01 Notes

  • Designing mobile first is not an instinct because we are designing and developing on a large screen, not mobile.
  • Design to your audience.
  • Keep things simple and clean.
  • Make it easy for the user.
  • Think about ergonomics of the device when designing.
  • The majority of people don't have the latest and greatest, so don't count on it.
  • Make it obvious what buttons/menus are and do.
